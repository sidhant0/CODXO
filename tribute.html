TRIBUTE TO
EMIWAY BANTAI

INTRODUCTION

As a Scorpio, Emiway showed an early inclination towards music and eventually founded his own music label, The Bantai Studio. This move marked the beginning of his journey as an independent artist in the Indian hip-hop scene.

LIFE AND ACHIEVEMENTS

Emiway Bantai quickly gained recognition in the Indian music industry through his compelling and catchy tracks. Some of his most notable songs include "Samajh Mein Aaya Kya," "Giraftaar," and "Khatam." These hits not only elevated his status as a major artist in the hip-hop community but also helped amass a staggering 21 million subscribers on his YouTube channel. The success of his music is evident as his top five most-viewed videos each boast over 10 million views, showcasing his widespread popularity and appeal.

LEGACY

In his personal life, Emiway often shares moments with his family, like wishing his father a Happy Birthday on his social media platform in June 2018, which gives his fans a glimpse into his off-stage life. The artist's influence and reach extended beyond just music when he made a cameo appearance in the 2019 film "Gully Boy," directed by renowned filmmaker Zoya Akhtar. This appearance linked him with the broader Indian entertainment industry and highlighted his versatility as an artist.
